ConsumerLogistic

Digital e-Commerce Platform to enable multiple retailers manage custom retail operations


What: An e-Commerce digital platform for various retail outlets to quickly provision their customized e-Commerce portal.

Integrated with advanced sales prediction and e-Commerce features, it provides an intuitive way to manage and plan for sales. One of the first users for this platform is “Bookmarcs Online”.

As part of their Digital Transformation journey, Bookmarcs will be enhancing its existing online presence with a new custom analytics based portal, that will provide a human-centred design based fully functional and effective website, along with a seamless backend admin capability to improve sales opportunities. The engagement also established a commercial (digital) platform that will not only provide Bookmarcs the ability to seamlessly manage and edit website content, but also be able to manage commercial sales aspects via an in-house capability.

Technologies Used: .Net Core, Azure, APIM, Angular, Azure Functions and AKS, Azure SQL

About the client: BookMarcs is a group professional booksellers and enthusiastic book collectors who have been selling books online since 1997. They are the first client to onboard Entelika’s e-Commerce Platform “EntelComm”.
