Strategy and Service Design

    
        

         Global pandemic has reiterated two philosophies
         of life - First, Change is the only constant 
         (and can come absolutely unannounced). Second, 
         only resilient organizations are capable to 
         navigate sudden market transformations.

         At Entelika, we constantly strive to calibrate how
         will our client’s business cope with a transformation.
         As such, Digital Strategy and Service Design is an 
         integral function of our service offering. 
         Entelika’s Service Design service involves applying 
         Design Thinking methodology into the creation of newer 
         services models for our clients - newer business models 
         that are innovative, economic, optimized and 
         deliver more value.

         Our Digital Strategy and Service Design (proprietary) 
         framework allows enterprises to mitigate all risks while 
         completely transforming the operating procedure of their 
         core business function(s). We assist with market research,
         business model framework,digital transformation strategy, 
         organizational change management approach and success
         measurement  frameworks.
    

    
        
Operational Excellence

    
                

       Entelika appreciates that a digital software product spends 80-85% of its
       lifetime in the operational part of the product life cycle while only 15-20% 
       in the development cycle. As such, it is critical to focus on Site Reliability
       Engineering aspects during the development cycle.

       Entelika uses Agile (Scrum and KanBan) methodology to work in a DevOps
       manner. This allows client and Entelika teams to collaboratively work on 
       Service Operations and Continuous Improvement aspects during the 
       product engineering phase. Entelika has teams across the world - in 

       Australia and India (besides the US) - that allows Entelika to provide 24X7 
       support model to organizations and follow SAFe methodology. Our team 
       members working on IT Operations are certified on ITSM frameworks like
       ITIL, COBIT, DevOps etc.
